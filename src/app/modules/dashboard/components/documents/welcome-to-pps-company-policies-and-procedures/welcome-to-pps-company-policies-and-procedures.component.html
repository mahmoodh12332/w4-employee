Welcome to PPS Company Policies and Procedures

BELOW IS A LIST OF IMPORTANT FACTS TO HELP YOU BE SUCCESSFUL IN YOUR ENDEAVORS. PLEASE READ THEM CAREFULLY. IF YOU HAVE ANY QUESTIONS, PLEASE ASK FOR HELP.

1.

NO CALL/NO SHOW/NO JOB!!!If you are unable to report to an assignment or wish to cancel, you must call Pacesetter Personnel Services two (2) hours prior to the start time of a job assignment. All no calls/no shows are subject to immediate termination.

2.

If PACESETTER PERSONNEL SERVICES does not dispatch you to a jobsite, then PACESETTER PERSONNEL SERVICES is not obligated to pay hours accrued at the jobsite. PACESETTER PERSONNEL SERVICES is not responsible for paying you for the hours that you worked at that jobsite.

3.

DO NOT CONTACT PACESETTER PERSONNEL SERVICES CLIENTS. Any questions should be directed to PACESETTER PERSONNEL SERVICES regarding assignment or interviews. All grievances will be handled through Pacesetter Personnel Services.

4.

DO follow all policies and procedures of Pacesetter Personnel Services and the client to which you are assigned.

5.

Personal cell phones and other handheld personal devices (tablets, laptops, gaming, etc.) are not to be used during scheduled work hours. If you need to use your personal cell phone or other hand held devices do it during an authorized break or during lunch break. Please informyour family and friends about this policy. Some flexibility may be given in case of an emergency that requires immediate attention. PPS is not responsible for lost or stolen phones or other devices. Keep in mind, some customer prohibit the use of cell phones and/or other devices on their sites.

6.

All Pacesetter Personnel Services employees are subject to random or reasonable suspicion drug and alcohol screening at any time. Failure to submit to the screening is immediate grounds for termination

7.

Pacesetter Personnel Services discourages relatives and/or spouses working at the same job assignment.

8.

DO wear all safety equipment and uniforms assigned to you while on the job. You must follow instructions for the proper use of all equipment and all applicable safety procedures. You are responsible for all lost or damaged equipment, uniforms and reporting to your manager any malfunctioning or missing equipment. Pacesetter Personnel Services employees may be charged for any lost or destroyed equipment or uniforms on loan to them from Pacesetter Personnel Services or our Clients.

9.

As a condition of employment, you are required to follow all safety procedures, rules and safe work practices, to exercise due care in the course of your work, operate machines or equipment that you have been training on, wear all required PPS as assigned including, but not limited to, seat belts, gloves, safety shoes, goggles, hard hats, respirators and hearing protections whenever conditions make this equipment necessary.

10.

I understand that the paycheck I receive for hours worked is my responsibility. If I lose or damage my check, I understand that there is a 7 business day waiting period before I can obtain a replacement check. I understand that a $25.00 charge will be deducted from my replacement check for the “stop payment fee” on the lost or damaged check

11.

In case of an accident/incident, notify your supervisor andPacesetter Personnel Services immediately. Pacesetter Personnel Services has to be notified to approve any medical treatment. If you seek unapproved non-emergency medical treatment, you may be responsible for the charges. Employees must have a properly filed E-1, within 24 hours after an accident.

12.

WHEN YOUR ASSIGNMENT IS COMPLETED, YOU MUST REPORT FOR REASSIGNMENT DAILY IN PERSON. WORK IS AVAILABLE. UNEMPLOYMENT BENEFITS MAY BE JEOPARDIZED FOR FAILURE TO DO SO.

13.

YOU UNDERSTAND THAT IN THE EVENT YOU CANNOT MAKE IT TO A JOB ASSIGNMENT ON A SPECIFIED DATE AND TIME, YOU MUST CALL PACESETTER PERSONNEL SERVICES. WE HAVE AN ANSWERING SERVICE AND ARE REACHABLE 24 HOURS A DAY, 7 DAYS A WEEK. IF YOU FAIL TO CONTACT PACESETTER PERSONNEL SERVICES THIS WILL RESULT IN DISCIPLINARY ACTION, UP TO AND INCLUDING TERMINATION.

I UNDERSTAND THE WELCOME TO PACESETTER PERSONNEL SERVICES COMPANY POLICIES AND PROCEDURES, AND ACKNOWLEDGE THAT I AM SUBJECT TO FOLLOWING THESE RULES. I UNDERSTAND THAT BY VIOLATING THESE RULES I MAY BE REFUSED FUTURE ASSIGNMENTS AND EVEN HAVE MY EMPLOYMENT WITH PACESETTER PERSONNEL SERVICES TERMINATED.
