Cellular Phone Policy

Pacesetter Personnel Services is concerned for the health and well-being of its employees, customers, and members of the general public with whom we come in contact. Accidents may occur as the result of careless use of cellular phones, laptops, and tablets while driving. As a result, numerous states, counties, and municipalities have passed laws imposing bans on the use of cellular phones and other wireless communications devices while driving. Pacesetter Personnel Services has established the following policy with regard to your use of cellular phones, laptops, and/or tablets while driving.

In compliance with the Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration prohibiting the use of a hand-held cell phone by commercial truck drivers, bus drivers or anyone transporting nine (9) or more passengers, Pacesetter Personnel Services has the following policy for cell phone usage.

The following are conditions of employment for anyone driving a company or private vehicle while employed or representing Pacesetter Personnel Services:

  • You are to familiarize yourself with any and all applicable state or local laws governing cellular phone use while driving.
  • You may only use hands free devices while operating your vehicle, and only if the operation of such device does not pose a safety risk and does not violate federal, state or local laws.
  • While driving for Pacesetter Personnel Services, you are not to place any cellular phone calls to anyone while operating your motor vehicle, whether for business or otherwise. If an emergency call must be made, you are required to pull the vehicle over, out of the flow of traffic, before placing the call.
  • You are not to use your laptop or tablet while operating your vehicle under any circumstances.
  • Writing, sending, and/or reading text messages or instant messages, using or viewing social media, surfing the Internet, interacting with GPS, and reading and/or responding to emails is strictly prohibited while operating your vehicle for Pacesetter. This applies to any device whether the device is company-supplied or personally owned.
  • You should avoid or discontinue use of your cellular phones in adverse weather or traffic conditions
  • You are to refrain from engaging in stressful or emotional conversations while driving your vehicle on company business.
  • In addition to the above, you are not permitted to make or answer calls, whether for business or personal use, while operating heavy motorized equipment, electronic tools or machinery. Any use of a cellular phone, laptop, tablet, or any similar device while operating heavy motorized equipment, electronic tools or machinery is strictly prohibited.
  • Employees assigned to work at Auto Auctions are prohibited from making or answering calls, texts, using a device whether for business or personal use while operating vehicles.

Pacesetter Personnel Services does not assume responsibility for your use of cellular phones, laptops, tablets, or similar devices. Employees should carefully evaluate the situation(s) and exercise the appropriate level of caution when using cellular phones, laptops, tablets, or similar devices.

Employees who violate this policy will be subject to disciplinary action, up to and including immediate termination. Any fines and penalties, damages to person(s) or property, and legal costs associated with infractions of these policies are the sole responsibility of the driver.
