Sexual Harassment Policy

Pacesetter Personnel Services is an Equal Opportunity Employer that does not condone or tolerate any type of unlawful conduct in the workplace, such as sexual harassment, intimidation, hostility, or other inappropriate sexual behaviors by or toward its employees.

The Employment Contract you signed with Pacesetter clearly states that Pacesetter “is committed to the principle of a dignified work environment and prohibits all forms of harassment to include, but not limited to sexual, racial, religious or ethnic harassment. Anyone believing that they are the recipient of such harassment should notify the Company immediately.” Pacesetter takes this matter very seriously, and as a Pacesetter employee, it is your responsibility to avoid engaging in any inappropriate conduct or making any comments that others might find objectionable or offensive, including but not limited to conduct or comments of a sexual nature.

Types of inappropriate behavior include, but are not limited to, making cat calls, using suggestive language, unwanted touching, or sexually explicit jokes. Such conduct is inappropriate in the workplace, and if you are found engaging in any such behavior, you will be subject to immediate disciplinary action, up to and including the immediate termination of your employment.

Pacesetter Personnel Services does not condone or approve of unlawful conduct towards its employees. Given the nature of the employee placement business, you may be exposed to conduct or comments by individuals over whom Pacesetter Personnel Services may not have control. These individuals may say or do things that you find objectionable or offensive. If you experience any conduct or comments that you find objectionable or offensive, including but not limited to harassment, sexual harassment, or any other inappropriate conduct, NOTIFY YOUR SUPERVISOR OR BRANCH MANAGER IMMEDIATELY! You should do this regardless of whether the inappropriate conduct is engaged in by other Pacesetter employees or employees of the client or customer to whom you are assigned. If you report conduct that you find objectionable or offensive to your supervisor or branch manager and you do not think an appropriate response occurred, or if you are reporting inappropriate conduct on the part of your supervisor or branch manager, please contact Human Resources at (888) 529-0202 Ext. 240 or hr@pacesetterpersonnel.com.

As an employee of Pacesetter, it is critical that you report any such inappropriate behavior or conduct immediately, as your report will enable the company to promptly investigate and address your complaint or concern.

In the event Pacesetter Personnel Services determines that any prohibited conduct or harassment occurred, including but not limited to sexual harassment as discussed herein, appropriate disciplinary action will be taken against any Pacesetter employee or employees who engaged in such inappropriate conduct, up to and including the immediate termination of the employment of that person or those persons. Pacesetter may report the findings of its investigation to its client as appropriate.

Pacesetter Personnel Services WILL NOT TOLERATE any actions that constitute retaliation by managers, supervisors or employees against any Pacesetter employee who files a claim or complaint regarding any prohibited conduct, including but not limited to sexual harassment. An employee who reports sexual harassment will not be retaliated against or discriminated against because he or she files a complaint or report.
