Arbitration Agreement

PLEASE READ THIS AGREEMENT CAREFULLY. BY SIGNING THIS AGREEMENT, BOTH YOU AND THE COMPANY ARE WAIVING CERTAIN RIGHTS TO LITIGATE DISPUTES IN COURT.

As a condition of my application for employment, I, the undersigned, hereby agree that any and all disputes arising out of my employment with the Company (as identified below) shall be resolved solely by arbitration, pursuant to the Federal Arbitration Act.

I agree that this Arbitration Agreement (“Agreement”) shall govern and apply to the resolution of all claims and/or disputes between and among myself and the Company, including but not limited to claims concerning:

1)

Any federal, state, or local laws, regulations, rules, or statutes prohibiting employment discrimination (such as, without limitation, race, color, sex, national origin, age, disability, and/or religion), harassment, and/or retaliation;

2)

Any federal, state, or local laws, regulations, rules, or statutes regarding failure to hire or wrongful termination;

3)

Any alleged or actual agreement, contracts, or covenants (oral, written, or implied);

4)

Any claim for personal, emotion, physical, or economic injury; and

5)

Any and all other claims, injuries, or disputes related to or arising from my employment with the Company, including but not limited to claims pertaining to negligent hiring, negligent firing, negligent training, assault, invasion of privacy, drug testing, and all other employment torts.

However, this Agreement specifically excludes the following claims:

1)

Any claims that arise under the National Labor Relations Act or are otherwise within the jurisdiction of the National Labor Relations Board;

2)

Any claims for workers’ compensation or unemployment compensation benefits, except that claims of retaliation or discrimination connected with workers’ compensation claims are subject to arbitration; and

3)

Claims by the Company against me for injunctive relief to protect the Company’s and/or its client’s confidential information, trade secrets, or other proprietary information, including actions to enforce rights pursuant to the Confidentiality Agreement I have signed.

I agree all such disputes will be resolved by arbitration as my sole and exclusive remedy. I further agree that I will pursue any claim relating to my employment with the Company (or any of its subsidiaries or related entities) as an individual, and will not lead, join, or serve as a member of a class or group of persons bringing such a claim or lawsuit. I further agree that the arbitration shall be conducted before an arbitrator mutually selected by the parties. The parties agree the arbitration will be conducted using the Federal Rules of Civil Procedure and Federal Rules of Evidence. The parties further agree that the arbitrator may not award relief not requested by a party and may not award relief based on a cause of action or claim not available under existing Federal law or the state law in the state in which the employee is assigned to work. In the event the employee works in more than one state, the parties agree the arbitrator will rely on Federal laws or the laws of the State of Texas. I further agree the decision of the arbitrator shall in all instances be final and binding.

I understand and acknowledge that this agreement is issued with the authority of the Company and is binding on the Company. I understand and acknowledge that this agreement will become effective on me and on the Company after I sign it and accept any offer of employment from or with the Company. I understand and acknowledge that the Company also agrees to arbitrate, in the same manner, any claims that the Company believes it has against me which arise from or relate to my employment with the Company.

I understand and agree that the Company and I shall share the cost of any such arbitration equally, unless the arbitrator or arbitrators rule otherwise. I understand that I am responsible for attorney’s fees and costs that I incur, unless the arbitrator or arbitrators rule otherwise.

MY SIGNATURE BELOW ATTESTS TO THE FACT THAT I HAVE READ, UNDERSTAND, AND AGREE TO BE LEGALLY BOUND TO ALL OF THE ABOVE TERMS. I FURTHER UNDERSTAND THAT THIS AGREEMENT REQUIRES ME TO ARBITRATE ANY AND ALL DISPUTES THAT ARISE OUT OF MY EMPLOYMENT.
